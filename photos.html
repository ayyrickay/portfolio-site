--- layout: default css: workpage title: PHOTOGRAPHS permalink: /photos/ infotext: "I first got interested in photography because of the idea of photovoice from anthropology. In the Fall of 2013 I got a DSLR, started fiddling with the settings, and have been taking photos ever since. I tend to approach my photos from an exploratory perspective; rather than trying to set up scenes, I'm more interested in going on a hike and trying to find out what exists in the world, and hopefully capture it from an interesting perspective.

Photography has taught me valuable lessons about design, primarily in the areas of composition, color, and presence. The eye of a photographer is always searching for a great scene, after all, and it doesn't just shut off. Now, when I sit down to build a prototype or design a flyer, I find that same eye working to try and generate the images that I would otherwise find out in the world." ---
